# Never open the life-map fit to all pins; open on the home pin at city zoom.

Correction (2026-07-02): the previous version of this lesson recommended passing `bounds` to the MapLibre constructor. That fixed the tile burst but was WRONG for the product: the owner's pins span continents, so fitting all pins IS the world view he complained about. The confirmed model is Snapchat's: construct the map centered on the `kind === 'home'` place (fallback: most recent by `arrived_on`, then Lagos) at `zoom: 10`, and let visitors pan outward. `fitBounds` must not appear in `LifeMap.astro` at all. The parts that still stand: set the view in the constructor (never reframe after load), DPR-aware @2x tiles, `minZoom: 2, maxZoom: 15`, IntersectionObserver lazy-init.
